Other Culture Food

From my traveling experiences to my time as an exchange student in Kazakhstan during high school and now as an international student at a US college, here are some of the foods from other cultures that I think are a must-try if you haven't!

Beshbarmak

Click this card to learn more!

Beshbarmak

National dish of Kazakhstan! Beshbarmak features boiled horse meat, served over wide, flat noodles with a rich onion sauce. It's perfect for a big meal with family and friends!

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

If you’re looking for a taste of Kazakhstan, you’ve got to try beshbarmak! Though lean, horse meat tastes surprisingly good! The noodles and sauce make it a really hearty meal, especially during the cold season.

Jjampong

Click this card to learn more!

Jjampong

A Korean staple, Jjampong is a spicy noodle soup that’s bursting with flavor. It's loaded with seafood like squid, shrimp, and mussels, along with veggies and chewy noodles, all simmered in a rich, spicy broth.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

If you’re love spicy food, jjampong is a must-try! This Korean noodle soup is packed with flavor and heat. The combination of fresh seafood with chewy noodles and a rich, spicy broth makes each bite incredibly satisfying.

Detroit Style Pizza

Detroit Style Pizza

Square-shaped pie that is known for its thick, airy crust that's crispy on the edges and soft in the middle. It's baked in a special deep pan, which gives it that unique texture and caramelized cheese edges.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

It is in my opinion better than the Chicago style deep dish pizza! Each bite is a perfect combination of chewy, crispy, and cheesy goodness. Definitely a must-try for any pizza lover!

English Breakfast

English Breakfast

English Breakfast includes crispy bacon, savory sausages, eggs, baked beans, grilled tomatoes, mushrooms, and toast. It also includes black pudding, a type of sausage made from pork blood, oats, and a blend of spices

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

If you're looking for a big meal for breakfast, this is it! The variety in an English breakfast will fulfill every single craving you have in the morning.

Irish Coffee

Irish Coffee

A mix of strong hot coffee, a shot of smooth Irish whiskey, a bit of brown sugar for sweetness, and topped with a layer of lightly whipped cream. The combination of flavors—bitter coffee, rich whiskey, sweet sugar, and creamy topping—makes for a deliciously indulgent treat.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

As someone who doesn’t like alcohol, I was pleasantly surprised by how amazing Irish coffee is! The whiskey blends so well with the coffee that it adds a nice warmth without being overpowering.

Welcome to Kai's Website!

Hey! My name is Kai, an international student studying at the University of Michigan. Growing up, I have always been a foodie! From the bold, aromatic flavors of Southeast Asia to the delicate dishes of East Asian cuisine and the widely enjoyed comfort food of the Americas... I love them all!

Thus, inspired by my passion for food and my desire to introduce Malaysian culture, I have decided to create this website!

I will introduce you to some of my favorite foods from both the Malaysian culture and other cultures, while providing you with my most honest opinions on them. I will also tell you more about Malaysia!
